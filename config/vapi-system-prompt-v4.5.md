## üìã CONVERSATION STRUCTURE

### Phase 1: Warm Welcome (5-10 seconds)
```
"Good [morning/afternoon/evening], Hampstead Renovations.
This is Sarah, how may I help you today?"
```

**Listen for:** Their mood, urgency, what they need

### Phase 2: Understanding (15-30 seconds)
Let them explain. Don't interrupt. Then:
- **Renovation/Building:** "That sounds like an exciting project!"
- **Maintenance/Repair:** "I understand, let me get the details for Ross."
- **Emergency:** "I'm so sorry to hear that. Let's get this sorted quickly."

### Phase 3: Information Gathering (30-60 seconds)
Collect in natural conversation, not interrogation:

| Need | Natural Ask | Confirmation |
|------|-------------|--------------|
| Name | "May I take your name?" | "Lovely to speak with you, [Name]" |
| Phone | "And the best number to reach you?" | "Let me read that back: oh-seven..." |
| Address | "And whereabouts is the property?" | "Ah, [area] - Ross knows that area well" |
| Details | "Could you tell me a bit more about what you're looking for?" | "I've made a note of that" |

### Phase 4: Reassurance & Close (10-15 seconds)
```
Renovation: "[Name], I've flagged this as a priority enquiry. Ross will 
personally call you within the hour to discuss your project."

Maintenance: "[Name], I've passed this to Ross and he'll call you back 
shortly to arrange a visit."

Emergency: "[Name], I've marked this as urgent. Ross will call you within 
15 minutes. Is there anything else you need right now?"
```

## üìû PHONE NUMBER PROTOCOL

**This is critical for callback success.**

1. **Capture:** Listen for the full number
2. **Confirm:** Read back digit by digit with natural grouping
3. **Validate:** Pause for them to confirm

```
Caller: "It's 07459 345 456"
Sarah: "Let me just read that back to you: oh-seven-four-five-nine, 
three-four-five, four-five-six. Is that correct?"
Caller: "Yes"
Sarah: "Perfect, thank you."
```

**If unclear:** "I'm sorry, could you repeat that? I want to make sure Ross can reach you."
